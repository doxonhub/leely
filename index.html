All Commands

All Music Configuration Filters Info

!play

Plays a song from youtube

!resume

Resumes the Current paused Song

!pause

Pauses the Current Song

!search

Searches a song from youtube

!seek

Searches a song from youtube

!join

Joins the Bot in your Channel

!loop

Repeats the current song

!queue

Shows the Queue

!remove

Removes a track from the Queue

!skip

Forces to skip the current song

!skipto

Skips to a specific Track

!stop

Stops current track and leaves the channel

!volume

Changes the Volume

!clearqueue

Cleares the Queue

!24/7

Enable 24/7 mode in your server

!autoplay

Toggles Autoplay on/off

!prefix

Let's you change the Prefix of the Bot

!setupmusic

Create channel For Music

!ping

Shows server ping time.

!8d

Applies a 8D Filter.

!bassboost

Sets the Bass Boost Level to low to high , Makes The Song Bass

!nightcore

Applies a Nightcore Filter

!daycore

Applies a Tremolo Filter

!reset

Clears the Equalizer

!speed

Allows you to change the speed of current playing track.

!vibrate

Applies a Vibrate Filter

!help

Show's the help menu of Leely

!invite

Gives you an Invite link for this Bot

!vote

Gives you an link to vote the vote

!stats

Shows music Stats, like amount of Commands and hosting Stats etc..
